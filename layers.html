Layers

The following data layers are used in Ocean Health Index calculations for goal status, trend, pressures, and resilience. Values for Global were extracted from the global assessment and saved for each region to serve as a template as you gather higher-resolution data and indicators. Note that many values do not have a range because they were equally extracted from the global data layer.

ao_access

Artisanal fisheries management effectiveness and opportunity

The opportunity for artisanal and recreational fishing based on the quality of management of the small-scale fishing sector

ao_need

Economic need for artisanal fishing

Per capita purchasing power parity (PPP) adjusted gross domestic product (GDP): GDPpcPPP as a proxy for subsistence fishing need

Ocean acidification

Ocean acidification pressure scaled using biological thresholds

Sea level rise

Sea level rise pressure

UV radiation pressure

Modeled UV radiation based on Erythemal UV Irradiance data provided by GES DISC.

Lookup table of country codes (based on ISO 3166 country codes) and Ocean Health Index region idenifiers.
